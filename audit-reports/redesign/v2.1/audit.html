PHASE 2 — SITE INTELLIGENCE

Investor-Grade Analysis

What they do Design & develop autonomous unmanned cargo helicopters for long-range logistics.
Industry Aerospace / Deep Tech / B2B Logistics
Target Audience Defence Logistics, Remote Supply Chain, Investors, Gov Agencies.
Company Stage Growth / Pre-Series (R2 Prototype established, R3 in development).
Tech Stack Next.js / Sanity CMS / Vercel / Tailwind CSS

The company presents as a highly sophisticated aerospace startup. The visual language is cinematic and premium, targeting high-value contracts and institutional investors. However, there are notable "cracks" in the content substance—specifically placeholder assets and missing conversion links—that suggest the site is recently updated or still in a hardening phase.

Page-by-Page Audit

Home Page

/
UI: 9/10 UX: 8/10 CONTENT: 6/10 TRUST: 7/10
Critical
Broken Conversion Path (Brochure)

The primary CTA "Download Brochure" in the hero section links to /enlite-brochure.pdf which returns a 404. For an investor-grade site, a broken PDF link in the hero is a major credibility killer.

Major
Inconsistent Asset Branding

The video and image assets on the homepage show "AURORA" and "AERO-CARGO 420" branding on the aircraft. This conflicts with the company name "Enlite" and product names "R2". Users may perceive the product as a white-labeled or unbranded prototype.

Good
Interactive Range Engagement

The "Interactive Range Map" is a world-class engagement feature. It provides immediate value and technical proof of concept for logistics planners.

The Fleet (R2 / R3)

/helicopters
UI: 9/10 UX: 8/10 CONTENT: 5/10 TRUST: 4/10
Major
Spec Sheets vs Visuals

The R2 page claims 70kg payload, but the accompanying visual is clearly a 3D render labeled as "AERO-CARGO". There is a lack of high-resolution "real" hardware photography, which is critical for the "Defence" and "Investor" audiences.

Minor
Duplicate SEO Title

The R2 and R3 pages share the exact same title tag as the homepage: "Enlite Helicopters — Autonomous Unmanned Cargo Helicopters". This creates internal competition for rankings (cannibalization).

Sitemap Domain Mismatch

The sitemap at /sitemap.xml lists all URLs as enlitehelicopters.com. However, the site is currently being served on enlite-helicopters.vercel.app. This confuses crawlers and splits authority between two domains. Canonical tags should be enforced.
